# Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Thanks for considering to contribute code to the PlanarMechanics Library. Before DLR can accept your contribution, you must sign and return the [Contributor License Agreement (CLA)](docs/DLR_IndividualContributorAgreement_PTL.pdf).

## How to sign the CLA:
1. Download the CLA: [Download CLA (PDF)](docs/DLR_IndividualContributorAgreement_PTL.pdf)
2. Complete the document.
3. Sign it by hand (including date).
4. Scan the signed document or take a clear photo.
5. Send the signed PDF or image to the e-mail address referenced in the document. 
6. Or optionally: print the signed document and send it to the DLR's postal address referenced in the document.

Once we receive your signed CLA, we will review your pull request.

> ⚠️ **Note:** We cannot merge your pull request until we have received your signed CLA.
